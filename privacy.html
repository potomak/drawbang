Privacy

Drawbang is a 16×16 pixel-art editor with an anonymous public gallery. There are no accounts, no logins, and no advertising profiles we build. Two third-party analytics tools are loaded on every page so the operator can understand how the site is used; you can disable both with one click below.

What we collect

  • Google Analytics 4. Pageviews and a small set of named events (which buttons you click, which products you preview). Loaded from googletagmanager.com; data ends up in Google's analytics service in the US. Sets a few cookies (_ga, _ga_*) that identify your browser to GA across pages on this site.
  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel. Pageviews plus two ecommerce events (ViewContent, InitiateCheckout). Loaded from connect.facebook.net; data ends up in Meta's pixel service. Sets the _fbp cookie.
  • Order details — only when you place an order. Your shipping address, email, and the Stripe session id, stored in the operator's AWS account (drawbang-orders table). Used to deliver the merch you bought and to send status emails. Never shared with the analytics tools above.

What we don't collect

  • No fingerprinting or persistent identifiers beyond the cookies Google and Meta set for their own tools.
  • No personal information in the analytics events — drawing ids and product ids are public, your name and email never appear there.
  • No advertising audience that we sell or pass on.

How to opt out

Pick either path — both fully disable Google Analytics and the Meta Pixel on the next page load. With opt-out on, neither script loads, neither cookie gets set, and no event leaves your browser.

  • Browser setting. Flip Do Not Track on in your browser. Drawbang honours it.
  • Per-device toggle. Click the button below. The choice is stored in this browser's localStorage under drawbang:analytics_opt_out and persists until you clear site data.
